About e-Reklamo

The e-Reklamo System is Barangay Lapasan's official digital platform for filing, tracking, and resolving community complaints. It was developed to bridge the gap between residents and the barangay government through technology.

Instead of visiting the barangay hall in person for every concern, residents can now submit complaints online 24/7 and receive real-time updates on the status of their reports. Officials can manage, prioritize, and resolve issues more efficiently through the Official Dashboard.

Our History

Barangay Lapasan through the years

1900s
Lapasan established as a sitio along the coastal areas of Cagayan de Oro, known for its fishing communities.
1945
Officially recognized as a barangay following post-war administrative reorganization of Cagayan de Oro City.
1990s
Rapid urbanization transforms Lapasan into a densely populated urban barangay with commercial and residential growth.
2020s
Barangay Lapasan embraces digital governance with the launch of the e-Reklamo complaint management system.
2026
e-Reklamo v2.0 launched - featuring AI-assisted categorization, real-time tracking, analytics dashboard, and multilingual support.
