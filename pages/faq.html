edit_document Filing a Complaint
How do I file a complaint?+
Click Login / Sign Up on the homepage, create a resident account, then go to File Complaint. Fill in the 2-step form with your personal information and complaint details. You will receive a unique Complaint ID after submission.
Do I need an account to file a complaint?+
Yes, you need to create a free resident account to file a complaint. This ensures accountability and allows you to track your complaint's status. Sign up takes less than 2 minutes.
Can I attach photos to my complaint?+
Yes! In Step 2 of the complaint form, you can upload photos (PNG, JPG) or PDF documents as evidence. Simply click the upload area or drag and drop your files.
What types of complaints can I file?+
You can file complaints about: Garbage / Waste Management, Noise, Road and Infrastructure issues, Peace and Order, Flooding / Drainage, Street Lights, Health / Sanitation, Illegal Structures, Environmental concerns, Animal Control, and other community concerns.
What is the urgency level and which should I choose?+
Low - Minor inconvenience, not time-sensitive.
Medium - Needs attention within a week.
High - Urgent, affecting daily life.
Critical - Immediate danger or safety risk. For true emergencies, always call the hotlines directly.
manage_search Tracking Your Complaint
How do I track my complaint?+
Visit the Track Complaint page and enter your Complaint ID (e.g., RPT-1234567890) or the email address you used when filing. You'll see the real-time status and progress of your complaint.
What do the complaint statuses mean?+
Pending Review - Just received, waiting for an official to review it.
Under Review - An official is currently examining your complaint.
In Progress - Action is being taken to resolve the issue.
Resolved - The issue has been addressed.
Closed - The complaint has been officially closed.
How long does resolution take?+
Typical resolution times depend on urgency: Critical issues are targeted within 1 day, High within 3 days, Medium within 7 days, and Low within 14 days. Complex infrastructure issues may take longer.
I lost my Complaint ID. What do I do?+
No problem - you can also track your complaint using the email address you registered with. Just enter it in the Track Complaint page. Alternatively, log in to your account and go to My Complaints.
verified_user Account & Privacy
Is my personal information secure?+
Yes. Your personal information is only accessible to authorized barangay officials and is used solely for the purpose of processing your complaint. We do not share your data with third parties.
Can I update my account information?+
Currently, account information is stored locally. If you need to update your information, please contact the barangay office directly at barangaylapasan@example.ph or call 09678770251.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?+
Click Forgot Password on the login page and enter your email address. You will receive instructions to reset your password.
report_problem Emergencies
Should I use e-Reklamo for emergencies?+
No. For life-threatening emergencies, always call the appropriate hotline directly: Fire Station (09756094097), Police Station 3 (09052149550), Tanod Hotline (09550482473), or 911. e-Reklamo is for non-emergency community concerns.
